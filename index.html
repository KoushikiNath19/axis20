About Axis'20

YOU NEED TO SEE THIS

AXIS is the annual technical festival of the Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur where students organize events, workshops and guest lecturers to impart technical expertise.AXIS organizes over 30 events, exhibitions and workshops, guest lectures. Every year, eminent dignitaries are invited to inaugurate the fest. In 2014, President of India Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam had graced us with his presence. Last year, A S Kirankumar, Chairman of ISRO was our honoured guest during the inauguration. The demand for innovation and practical knowledge is the main driving force behind AXIS.
